Welcome

Coronavirus Index (CoV Index) is a map of coronavirus research and researchers. CoV Index work uses network analysis, bibliometrics, and computational linguistics to identify communities of Coronavirus research. The primary goal of CoV Index is to organize Coronavirus research so scientists from other disciplines can engage with the Coronavirus research community.
