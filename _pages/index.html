Coronavirus Index (CoVIndex) is a project that maps networks of coronavirus research and researchers. CoVIndex is a resource for discovering new connections in the literature.

Interdisciplinary Coronavirus Research

For decades, Coronavirus research has occurred in the biological sciences and, to some extent, public health/epidemiology - but now other kinds of researchers are paying attention. With the outbreak of COVID-19, Coronavirus research is likely to encompass many new disciplines due to the impact Coronavirae have upon society, economy, politics, and more.

CoVIndex bridges the gaps between academic disciplines. In this project, we analyze networks of articles and researchers to build an index of interconnected Coronavirus resources.
